Why a retreat?

The Speck&Tech Retreat is THE event you can't miss! On December 2-4, our Speckers will reunite to strengthen their relationships, learn from each other, have fun, discuss together trending hot topics and spend an unforgettable weekend with old and new friends.

In 2018 over 50 members gathered in Dimaro for a great 3-day event; they became more than 60 one year later, in 2019. This year we want to bring that festive spirit again after two years of pandemic, and - as always - we offer you a broad range of activities that will not only boost your career from a professional and personal point of view, but will also allow you to take a short holiday with the people you love, surrounded by a stunning environment and relaxing views.

The programme covers a multitude of distinct areas, from technical and stimulating talks to enriching workshops, from outdoor activities in the Alps to long relaxing hours in SPA and sauna, from gamified visits of top tech companies to tasty gourmet dinners.

FAQs

- Who can attend the event and what are the selection criteria?

Everyone is welcome! However, you must be at least 18 years old for legal and liability reasons. As there are limited spots available, we strongly recommend that you register as soon as possible: participants will be granted a spot on a first-come, first-served basis. The Organizing team reserves the right to approve each participant.

- What is required in order to participate?

You are requested to complete the registration form for the Eventbrite event, and pay a deposit (which partially covers your participation fee, and will be returned at show-up after the event).

- Why do I have to pay a deposit?

We ask you for a deposit in order to confirm your participation in the event, so we can fully rely on your registration, plan accordingly and provide you with the best event experience.

- What happens to my deposit after the event?

The deposit will be fully reimbursed once you show up at the event. If you miss the event, you will lose your deposit. You will pay your participation fee directly to the hotel.

- What happens if I cannot attend the event after being accepted?

You can cancel your registration until 30 days prior to the event start (i.e. November 2), and you will immediately get the entire deposit back. Any cancellation after the deadline for reasons of force majeure will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

- What if I am not one of the first 50 successful applicants?

Given the limited spots available, we cannot guarantee the participation of each member. In case all tickets are sold out, a waiting list will be set up. If a ticket becomes available, you will be contacted automatically with further instructions on how to get it.

- Can I choose with whom to stay in my room?

Please, do mention whom you would like to share your room with directly on the registration form for the Eventbrite event. We will do our best to fulfill all requests.

- Which outdoor activity may I attend? Do I have to attend one?

- What should I bring?

Regardless of the outdoor activity you will choose for Sunday afternoon, we suggest you bring a swimsuit, mountain shoes, and comfortable and warm clothes. All events are casual.

Not really. The event comes as a whole. Partial attendance for reasons of force majeure will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
